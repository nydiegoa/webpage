How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich:

1.    You’ll need bread, a jar of peanut butter, a jar of jelly, a knife, a spoon, and a plate.
2.    Take out 2 slices of bread and place them on the plate next to one another.
3.    Open your jar of peanut butter.
4.    With your knife take some peanut butter and spread it on one of the slices of bread.
5.    Open your jar of jelly.
6.    With your spoon, take some jelly and spread it on the other slice of bread.
7.    Put the 2 slices together.
8.    Cut the sandwich in half with the knife.
9.    Close the jars; seal up the bread.
10.    Put the bread, peanut butter, and jelly away (Mom will love you!)
11.    Put the knife and spoon in the sink to wash later with your plate.
12.    Get a glass of milk.
13.    Enjoy your lunch!
